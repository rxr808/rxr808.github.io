How To Make Thrifting Trendy

Have you ever wondered how to go shopping sustainably while on a budget but still being on trend? Many people have the misconception that it is virtually impossible to attain all of these at the same time. Little do they know that thrifting solves all these problems and can be such a fun experience! Ready to find out how to go thrifting and be on trend?

Go to the Vogue Runway website and pay attention to the trends in each designer's collection. Once you've noted what is popular, write them down.

You know how you make a grocery list before a shopping trip? It's the same when you shop for clothes! Write down whether you want to look for a specific style of pants or jacket to add to your closet--or whether you're missing an essential piece.

Once you get to the thrift store, take out your handy dandy notebook and scan what you wrote down. Then keep what you read, in mind and search for that in the store!

Make sure that the piece you are getting is in good condition and isn't totally worn out or broken to the point where you can no longer fix it.

Head over to the fitting room, if there is one, to avoid picking up items that don't fit.
